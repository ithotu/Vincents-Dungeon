ABOUT VINCENT

I'm Vincent, a 21 year old transgender male from the United States. I use it/itself or certain neopronouns. He/him is acceptable but low on the list of preference.

I'm an art history major looking to go to graduate school for critical and curatorial studies. My focus is on modern art, particularly modern queer art. Some of my favourite artists are Robert Mapplethorpe, David Wojnarowicz, Zoe Leonard, Yishay Garbasz, Tom of Finland, Glenn Ligon, and Essex Hemphill.

I create art of all types: I do nature photography, I write both prose and poetry, I draw and digitally paint. Art is a large focus of my life, even though I don't create as much as I would like.

Outside of art, I have several other hobbies. Web development is one. I enjoy playing around with Linux and ricing my computers. I've been a Linux user for some time now, and use it 99% exclusively with the 1% being dual-booting Windows for gaming. On that note, I play a lot of video games. Some of my most played games are Skyrim, Fallout: New Vegas, Minecraft, The Binding of Isaac: Repentance, and The Bioshock Series.

I love film, with horror being my favorite genre but I'll watch almost anything. Some of my favourite films are: Harold and Maude, The Exorcist, Clerks, My Own Private Idaho, Re-Animator, Watership Down, Hereditary, Funny Games, and Fight Club.

I'm also alterhuman and a furry. I have a fursona page here. My alterhuman type is that of a catboy, meaning a humanoid with cat features both behaviorally and physically. My alterhumanity is very important to my identity, so it will come up often.
