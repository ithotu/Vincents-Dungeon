Burnout and Other Stories

October 9th, 2022

I am worn the fuck out.

It's not even anything in particular this time. School. Work. Life. It's all just weighing down.

It's one of those periods of time where the entire world seems to be covered in a grey cloud. I'm tired. I'm depressed. I haven't felt this bad in a while. I'm going, that's all I can say. Not much good news to report, though.

Bit of a downer of a diary post, and I'm sorry for that (to the very few that will probably ever read this), just need to get some feelings out I guess. The weight on my chest will go away one day. I will breathe again. For now, though, its unfortunately further out than I would like.

Some good things. I have friends I like talking to. I have a job that pays pretty well. I have two cats and a tarantula I adore. I'm doing better than I thought I would at this age.

I wish I had any particular stories, but I don't. At least, if I did, I forgot whatever anecdotes I was going to tell. People are kind of annoying me overall, but I know a lot of it is just my pissy mood. The other part is definitely the fact that people are, in general, just annoying though.

Kinda just want a hug to be honest.
