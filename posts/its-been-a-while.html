It's Been A While

December 10th, 2022

It's been a while! Turns out, school likes to kick my ass and take out any and all motivation to do things besides sleep and do schoolwork. My semester is over though, so here's a long overdue life update. It's been two months and a day since my last update, so for ease of writing, I'm going to divide this blog post up into some general sections based on what I've been up to.

School

This upcoming semester is my last semester of undergrad, which is a bizarre feeling. I'm going to be going into graduate school, probably hopefully, but I still feel strange about everything. Nonetheless, let's focus on the past rather than the future for this moment.

School was difficult. It tends to be, as someone with ADHD that I don't manage as well as I could, but this semester felt especially hard. Got hit hard with depression and burnout, I think. I had one class that was particularly fucking obnoxious, if only because the professor never posted grades. I feel like there should be a rule that professors have to give me access to my grades without me having to ask. He was kind of a dick in emails so I tried not to email him much.

Got good grades on all my finals though, so I'm not too worried about that. I did a lot of my finals fairly last minute so I'm pleased that I managed to get some good scores.

Work

Work has been pretty good, actually. I didn't get that job I was hoping for, which sucked, but I have really good bosses. My supervisor and I cried together and hugged because she said a bunch of nice shit about me. I got a review within the past few days as well and that turned out good. I'm very appreciated.

My hours got cut for next semester though, which I'm peeved about. Not an issue with my work but more of a budget issue with the university, apparently. Dunno the full sitaution, but it sucks since I've started to actually build up a nice chunk of money for myself. Just gonna keep saving.

My coworkers have been annoying me a bit too, but that's par for the course I guess. I don't hate anyone, just some minor things.

Fun

I've had a deep, embarrassing obsession with Pokemon the past while. I haven't even gotten Scarlet and/or Violet (still waiting for them to patch that fucking nightmare) but I have been replaying some old games. Been trying to get into Nuzlocking, but it's a pain in my left nut. I'm not 'good' at Pokemon, I care more about the collection aspect, but it is still fun to keep throwing myself at the wall sometimes. Took a break after the semester ended from Nuzlocking to just fuck around in games but going to start that up again soon. Really want to continue this Emerald one I was doing, since I've never actually played a Gen 3 game before. I know, sacriliege.

The whole Scarlet/Violet thing has deeply disappointed me, not going to lie. I know that Gamefreak is notorious for this, but this is the worst game that they've ever put out, but it's also clear that a lot of people really like the ideas that they explore within it. I want to like it. I know Pokemon is a marketing machine, but I can't really remove myself from how much the series means to me since I've been involved with it my whole life. It just really sucks that they put out a game this genuinely unfinished and bad and expect people to pay full price for it. Pay for it at all, really. I hope they patch it or something, because even if I end up not buying it, I at least want the people who essentially got scammed to have a better time with it.

Been playing Minecraft too, trying to get back into the Hardcore grind. I'm finding that the parts I find the most frustrating with Minecraft are the mining and building parts. I kinda wish I could just make a house out of thin air and start in that rather than having to fuck around with building since I just find it tedious. Close to having a house built in my latest world though, so let's hope that I don't just immediately get sniped by a skeleton as tends to be the end for all of my hardcore worlds.

I got two new tarantulas as well, a Mexican Red Rump named Barb and a G. Pulchra named Judith. They're very tiny at the moment. Also got my oldest tarantula, Evelyn, a giant cage upgrade. She's ballin', stupid manthing.

Anyways, that's it for this update. Smaller than I expected but life really has been just papers and stress. Hope I can talk more as I settle into being on break. See you soon!
